# Getting Started

*Part of our guide to [running an online community you actually own](/articles/how-to-run-an-online-community).*

To get started, visit [app.flotilla.social](https://app.flotilla.social). You'll be welcomed with two choices: **Log in**, if you've been here before, or **Create an account**, if you're new.

Click **Create an account** and you'll be offered two ways to sign up: the quick way, with an email and password, or by generating your own key. They lead to the same place — the difference is just who looks after your account's key. Pick whichever suits you; you can always switch later.

## Sign up with email

This is the simplest way in, and a good choice if you're new to all of this.

1. On the "Join Flotilla" screen, choose **Sign up with email**.
2. Enter your **email address** and a **password** (at least 12 characters). Behind the scenes, Flotilla still creates a real, self-owned identity for you — it just splits the key across several servers and locks it with your password, so it stays safe without you having to keep track of anything. As the app puts it: if you're not ready to take control of your keys yet, that's fine, and it'll hold them for you until you are.
3. Check your inbox for a **one-time confirmation code** and enter it to verify your email.
4. Fill out your **profile** — a name, a picture, a short bio. All of it is optional, so add as much or as little as you like, then continue.

That's it. From then on you can sign in on any device with your email and password (or a one-time code if you forget it), and you can graduate to holding your own key whenever you feel ready.

## Generate your own key

This path gives you full control from the start. Instead of an account a company issues and can revoke, you get a **key** that belongs to you: a *public* half that acts as your ID, and a *private* half that works like a master password. Because you hold it, no one can lock you out or take your identity away.

1. On the "Join Flotilla" screen, choose **Generate a key**. Flotilla creates a new key and stores it in your browser.
2. Fill out your **profile** — a name, a picture, a short bio, all optional — then continue.
3. On the "Your Keys are Ready!" screen, click **Download my key** to save a backup file. If you'd like extra protection, choose **"I want to download an encrypted version"** and set a password (at least 12 characters) first. You have to download the key before you can move on.

Keep that file somewhere safe, like a password manager — [Bitwarden](https://bitwarden.com/) is a good free option. This is the one step to take seriously: your key *is* your account, there's no "forgot password" reset, and if you lose it or it's stolen you lose access to your identity for good. The backup also lets you log in to other [Nostr apps](https://nostrapps.com) with the same profile.

## Already have an account?

If you've used Flotilla or another Nostr app before, choose **Log in** on the welcome screen instead and bring your existing key in. There are several ways to do that, and some are safer than others — see [Ways to Log In to Flotilla](/articles/logging-in-to-flotilla) for the full rundown, and [Understanding Your Nostr Identity](/articles/understanding-your-nostr-identity) if you'd like the bigger picture first.

## You're in

However you signed up, you'll land on your dashboard, ready to connect with your community — [join a space](/articles/joining-a-space) someone has invited you to, or [start one of your own](/articles/creating-a-space).

# What is Flotilla?

Flotilla is **software for sovereign communities**. It uses the nostr protocol to create a new kind of community group, which:

- Can be hosted on your own server, or use any service provider you choose.
- Makes it possible to implement your own custom moderation policies, bots, integrations, access controls, and more.
- Allows users to own their social identity, making it possible to move from group to group, or even to another platform, without losing anything.
- Takes advantage of conventional web technologies for performance and scaling.

Flotilla is designed to be easy to use, even if you don't know anything about nostr relays or cryptographic keys.
